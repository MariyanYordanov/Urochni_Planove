### **ЧАС 1: Основи на biometric authentication (40 мин)**

#### **Теоретична част (25 мин):**

**🧬 Какво са biometrics? (8 мин)**
- Дефиниция: измерване на уникални физически или поведенчески характеристики
- Историческа справка: 
  - Древен Китай - пръстови отпечатъци за документи (300 г. сл.н.е.)
  - Sir Francis Galton (1888) - класификация на fingerprints
  - Alphonse Bertillon (1882) - първия biometric identification система
- Модерно приложение: от криминалистика към масово използване

**🔐 Biometrics в authentication framework (10 мин)**
- **Enrollment процес:**
  1. **Capture:** снимане/записване на biometric sample
  2. **Feature extraction:** извличане на уникални характеристики  
  3. **Template creation:** създаване на digital template за съхранение
  4. **Database storage:** запазване на template (не raw data!)

- **Verification процес (1:1 matching):**
  1. **Capture:** нов biometric sample от потребител
  2. **Feature extraction:** обработка на новия sample
  3. **Comparison:** сравнение със stored template
  4. **Decision:** accept/reject based на matching threshold

- **Identification процес (1:N matching):**
  - Сравнение на sample с цяла database от templates
  - По-сложен и бавен от verification
  - Използва се за forensics, surveillance

**📊 Performance метрики (7 мин)**
- **False Accept Rate (FAR):** процент неправилно приети unauthorized users
- **False Reject Rate (FRR):** процент неправилно отхвърлени authorized users  
- **Equal Error Rate (EER):** точката където FAR = FRR (lower is better)
- **Crossover Error Rate (CER):** друго име за EER
- **Failure to Enroll Rate (FTE):** процент потребители, които не могат да се enrollment

*Примери за typical performance:*
- Fingerprint: EER ~ 2-5%
- Iris: EER ~ 0.1-0.01%  
- Face: EER ~ 1-10% (зависи от условията)
- Voice: EER ~ 2-5%

### **ЧАС 2: Fingerprint recognition технологии (40 мин)**

#### **Теоретична част (20 мин):**

**👆 Fingerprint science (8 мин)**
- **Уникалност:** всеки човек има уникални папиларни линии
- **Постоянство:** patterns не се променят през целия живот
- **Classification:** whorl, loop, arch patterns
- **Minutiae points:** ridge endings, bifurcations, dots
  - Типичен fingerprint има 150+ minutiae points
  - Matching algorithm търси 12-15 common points

**🔬 Capture технологии (8 мин)**

**Optical Scanning:**
- CCD/CMOS sensor снима finger surface
- Prism-based total internal reflection
- Предимства: висока resolution, proven technology
- Недостатъци: size, susceptible към dirt/moisture
- Cost: €50-200 per unit

**Capacitive Scanning:**
- Измерва electrical differences между ridges и valleys
- Semiconductor sensor array
- Предимства: compact, difficult да се spoof със снимки
- Недостатъци: sensitive на cuts/scars
- Използва се в smartphones, tablets

**Thermal Scanning:**  
- Детектира temperature differences
- Ridges са по-близо до sensors = по-топли
- Предимства: works със мокри пръсти
- Недостатъци: expensive, slower

**Ultrasonic Scanning:**
- Sound waves penetrate skin surface
- 3D mapping на fingerprint structure
- Предимства: works със мръсни/мокри пръсти, anti-spoofing
- Недостатъци: new technology, expensive
- Използва се в flagship smartphones (Samsung Galaxy S)

**⚖️ Matching algorithms и accuracy (4 мин)**
- **Template size:** обикновено 500-1000 bytes per fingerprint
- **Matching speed:** < 1 секунда за verification
- **Database scaling:** identification time increases с database size
- **Live finger detection:** anti-spoofing measures срещу fake fingers

### **ЧАС 3: Iris и retina scanning (40 мин)**

#### **Теоретична част (22 мин):**

**👁️ Iris recognition fundamentals (10 мин)**
- **Iris anatomy:** colored part на окото around pupil
- **Uniqueness factors:**
  - Complex pattern на fibers, crypts, furrows
  - Develops през 3rd-8th месец на бременността
  - Стабилен throughout life (освен при trauma/surgery)
  - Дори identical twins имат different iris patterns

- **Capture process:**
  - Near-infrared camera (700-900nm wavelength)  
  - Distance: 3cm до 1 meter от окото
  - Automatic pupil detection и iris boundary location
  - Polar coordinate transformation за normalization

**🌟 Iris vs. Retina scanning (8 мин)**

**Iris Recognition:**
- **What:** pattern на iris tissue
- **Capture:** external photography, non-invasive  
- **Distance:** work from 10cm до 1m away
- **Speed:** < 2 seconds capture и processing
- **User experience:** comfortable, no contact needed
- **Accuracy:** EER ~0.01% (най-accurate biometric)
- **Template size:** ~512 bytes
- **Applications:** airports, high-security facilities

**Retina Scanning:**
- **What:** blood vessel patterns в retina (back на окото)
- **Capture:** infrared light shined into eye
- **Distance:** must be very close към scanner (1-2 cm)
- **Speed:** 10-15 seconds за good capture
- **User experience:** uncomfortable, requires training
- **Accuracy:** EER ~0.0001% (extremely accurate)
- **Medical concerns:** potential eye damage от infrared light
- **Applications:** mostly discontinued due към user acceptance issues

**🚀 Advanced iris technology (4 мин)**
- **Iris on the move:** scanning walking людe
- **Long-distance iris:** capture from 1-2 meters
- **Mobile iris scanning:** smartphone integration
- **Anti-spoofing:** detection на printed eye photos
- **Dual-eye enrollment:** increased security със both eyes

*Real-world deployments:*
- **UAE borders:** всички visitors get iris scanned
- **Aadhaar program (India):** 1.3 billion iris templates stored  
- **US airports:** Global Entry program uses iris
- **ATMs в Japan:** iris-enabled cash machines

### **ЧАС 4: Face recognition systems (40 мин)**

#### **Теоретична част (24 мин):**

**😊 Face recognition основи (10 мин)**
- **Eigenfaces approach:** mathematical representation using principal components
- **Feature-based methods:** distance между очи, nose shape, jawline measurements
- **3D face recognition:** depth mapping за improved accuracy
- **Thermal face recognition:** heat patterns вместо visible light

**Key challenges:**
- **Lighting variations:** recognition accuracy drops significantly със poor lighting
- **Aging effects:** facial features change over time
- **Expression changes:** smiling vs. neutral може да affect matching  
- **Pose variations:** profile vs. frontal view matching difficulties
- **Occlusion issues:** glasses, masks, hair covering parts на лицето

**📷 2D vs. 3D face recognition (8 мин)**

**2D Face Recognition:**
- **Technology:** regular camera със software analysis
- **Processing:** pattern recognition на 2D image features
- **Pros:** inexpensive hardware, fast processing, established algorithms
- **Cons:** affected by lighting, makeup, facial hair changes
- **Accuracy:** EER 1-10% depending на conditions
- **Spoofing risk:** може да се fool със photos или videos
- **Applications:** smartphone unlock, social media tagging

**3D Face Recognition:**  
- **Technology:** structured light или stereo cameras за depth mapping
- **Processing:** geometric analysis на facial structure
- **Pros:** not affected от lighting, makeup, difficult да spoof
- **Cons:** expensive hardware, slower processing
- **Accuracy:** EER 0.1-1% под controlled conditions  
- **Hardware cost:** 5-10x more expensive от 2D systems
- **Applications:** border control, high-security access

**🤖 Modern AI developments (6 мин)**
- **Deep learning:** Convolutional Neural Networks за feature extraction
- **Face embeddings:** 128-512 dimensional vectors representing faces
- **Real-time processing:** mobile GPUs enable instant recognition
- **Mask detection:** COVID-19 adaptations за masked face recognition
- **Emotion recognition:** detecting mood/state from facial expressions

*Controversial applications:*
- **Mass surveillance:** China's social credit system
- **Retail analytics:** tracking customer behavior в stores  
- **Workplace monitoring:** attendance и productivity tracking
- **Law enforcement:** automatic identification в public spaces

### **ЧАС 5: Voice recognition и behavioral biometrics (40 мин)**

#### **Теоретична част (22 мин):**

**🗣️ Voice biometrics fundamentals (10 мин)**
- **Physiological factors:** vocal tract shape, larynx size, tongue/lip movement patterns
- **Behavioral factors:** accent, speaking rhythm, pronunciation habits  
- **Frequency analysis:** fundamental frequency (pitch) и harmonics
- **Spectral patterns:** unique voice "fingerprint" в frequency domain

**Voice processing pipeline:**
1. **Audio capture:** microphone recording (8kHz+ sampling rate)
2. **Noise filtering:** remove background sounds, echo
3. **Feature extraction:** mel-frequency cepstral coefficients (MFCC)
4. **Pattern matching:** compare със stored voiceprints  
5. **Decision logic:** threshold-based accept/reject

**Challenges in voice biometrics:**
- **Health effects:** cold, sore throat affects voice characteristics
- **Emotional state:** stress, excitement changes vocal patterns
- **Background noise:** affects capture quality и accuracy
- **Channel variations:** different microphones/phones change audio quality
- **Aging effects:** voice changes over decades

**🚶 Behavioral biometrics overview (8 мин)**

**Keystroke Dynamics (Typing Patterns):**
- **Dwell time:** how long keys are pressed down
- **Flight time:** time between releasing one key и pressing next  
- **Typing rhythm:** personal cadence и patterns
- **Applications:** continuous authentication, additional security layer
- **Accuracy:** EER ~2-10% depending на text length

**Gait Analysis (Walking Patterns):**  
- **Stride length:** distance covered per step
- **Walking speed:** consistent personal pace
- **Body movement:** hip rotation, arm swing patterns
- **Sensor data:** accelerometers, gyroscopes в smartphones
- **Applications:** healthcare monitoring, covert identification

**Signature Dynamics:**
- **Pressure patterns:** how hard pen is pressed
- **Speed variations:** fast/slow parts на signature
- **Pen lift patterns:** where/when pen leaves paper  
- **Applications:** document authentication, banking

**Mouse/Touchscreen Dynamics:**
- **Movement patterns:** how cursor/finger moves
- **Click/tap pressure:** force applied to interactions
- **Gesture patterns:** personal habits в UI navigation
- **Applications:** fraud detection, user profiling

**🧠 Modern developments (4 мин)**
- **Multimodal behavioral fusion:** combining multiple behavioral patterns
- **Machine learning improvements:** deep learning за pattern recognition
- **Continuous authentication:** ongoing identity verification
- **Privacy-preserving techniques:** local processing, federated learning

### **ЧАС 6: Privacy, ethics и практически приложения (40 мин)**

#### **Теоретична част (20 мин):**

**🔒 Privacy concerns with biometrics (8 мин)**
- **Permanent compromise:** you can't change your fingerprints like passwords
- **Centralized databases:** single breach exposes biometrics за millions
- **Cross-system tracking:** same biometric used across multiple services
- **Covert collection:** биометрични данни могат да се collect без knowledge

**GDPR и biometric data:**
- Classified as "special category personal data"  
- Requires explicit consent за processing
- Right to erasure - must delete биометрични templates on request
- Data portability requirements
- Impact assessments required за biometric systems

**Identity theft 2.0:**
- Traditional identity theft: steal credit cards, SSN
- Biometric theft: permanent identity compromise  
- **Deepfakes:** AI-generated faces за spoofing face recognition
- **Synthetic fingerprints:** artificially created prints that match multiple people

**⚖️ Ethical considerations (8 мин)**

**Consent и coercion:**
- **Employment requirements:** can employers require biometric enrollment?
- **Educational settings:** students forced да participate?  
- **Government services:** biometrics as requirement за benefits?
- **Informed consent:** do users understand long-term implications?

**Bias и discrimination:**
- **Algorithmic bias:** systems less accurate за certain ethnic groups
- **Age bias:** elderly people may have degraded biometrics
- **Disability discrimination:** some people cannot use certain biometric types
- **Gender bias:** different error rates за men vs. women

**Surveillance concerns:**
- **Function creep:** systems deployed за one purpose, used за others
- **Mass surveillance:** combining biometrics със ubiquitous cameras
- **Social control:** chilling effect на privacy и free expression
- **Democratic implications:** impact на protest, dissent, anonymity

**💼 Real-world deployment considerations (4 мин)**
- **Cost-benefit analysis:** expensive technology - worth the investment?
- **User acceptance:** employee/customer willingness да use systems  
- **Integration challenges:** compatibility със existing security systems
- **Maintenance overhead:** ongoing support, hardware replacement cycles
- **Legal compliance:** meeting regulatory requirements
