### **ЧАС 1: CCTV системи и компоненти (40 мин)**

#### **Теоретична част (25 мин):**

**📹 CCTV System Overview (10 мин)**
- **CCTV = Closed-Circuit Television**
- **Основни компоненти:**
  - **Cameras:** capture device за video recording
  - **Transmission:** cables, wireless, network infrastructure  
  - **Recording:** DVR/NVR systems, cloud storage
  - **Monitoring:** displays, control interfaces
  - **Management:** software за system control

**Историческо развитие:**
- **1942:** Първата CCTV система в Germany за monitoring V-2 rockets
- **1960s:** Commercial adoption в retail stores
- **1980s:** VCR integration за recording capabilities
- **2000s:** Digital revolution - DVR systems
- **2010s:** Network-based systems (IP cameras)
- **2020s:** AI-powered analytics integration

**🎥 Camera Technologies (8 мин)**

**Analog vs. Digital Cameras:**
- **Analog (CVBS):** traditional coaxial cable, standard definition
- **HD-over-Coax:** high definition през existing analog infrastructure  
- **IP Cameras:** network-based, higher resolution, advanced features
- **Hybrid Systems:** mixing analog и digital cameras

**Resolution Evolution:**
- **Standard Definition:** 720x480 (D1), adequate за basic identification
- **High Definition:** 1280x720 (720p), 1920x1080 (1080p)
- **Ultra High Definition:** 3840x2160 (4K), exceptional detail
- **Megapixel Options:** 2MP, 5MP, 8MP+ за specialized applications

**Camera Form Factors:**
- **Dome Cameras:** discrete, vandal-resistant, 360° positioning
- **Bullet Cameras:** visible deterrent, weather-resistant, directed view
- **PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom):** remote control, wide area coverage, detailed zoom
- **Hidden/Covert:** investigation applications, minimal visual impact

**🔌 System Architecture (7 мин)**

**Recording Systems:**
- **DVR (Digital Video Recorder):** analog cameras, local processing
- **NVR (Network Video Recorder):** IP cameras, network-based processing
- **Cloud Recording:** off-site storage, subscription-based services
- **Hybrid Recording:** mixed analog/digital support

**Storage Considerations:**
- **Capacity planning:** resolution × cameras × retention time
- **Redundancy:** RAID configurations, backup systems
- **Compression:** H.264, H.265 encoding за storage efficiency
- **Retention policies:** legal requirements, business needs

*Storage calculation example:*
```
10 cameras × 1080p × 24/7 recording × 30 days retention
= approximately 12TB storage needed
```

**Network Infrastructure:**
- **PoE (Power over Ethernet):** single cable за power + data
- **Bandwidth planning:** compressed video streams over network
- **Switch capacity:** handling multiple high-definition streams
- **Remote access:** secure viewing от mobile devices, web browsers

### **ЧАС 2: Video quality и performance factors (40 мин)**

#### **Теоретична част (22 мин):**

**🌟 Video Quality Factors (10 мин)**

**Resolution Impact:**
- **Pixels per foot:** how many pixels на subject at different distances
- **Identification requirements:** face recognition needs ~40 pixels across face
- **License plate reading:** needs ~130 pixels across plate width
- **General surveillance:** 20-30 pixels sufficient за human detection

**Lighting Considerations:**
- **Lux levels:** measurement на light intensity
  - **Bright sunlight:** 100,000 lux
  - **Office lighting:** 300-500 lux  
  - **Street lighting:** 10-20 lux
  - **Moonlight:** 0.1 lux
- **Camera sensitivity:** minimum lux rating за acceptable performance
- **Infrared illumination:** active IR LEDs за night vision

**Frame Rate (FPS) Requirements:**
- **Real-time monitoring:** 30 FPS за smooth motion
- **Playback investigation:** 15 FPS adequate за most applications
- **Storage optimization:** 10 FPS за long-term retention
- **Motion-triggered recording:** higher FPS during events only

**📊 Performance Metrics (8 мин)**

**Detection vs. Recognition vs. Identification:**
- **Detection:** "Something is there" - lowest resolution requirement
- **Recognition:** "That looks like a person I've seen before" - medium resolution
- **Identification:** "That is definitely John Smith" - highest resolution requirement

*Distance and resolution relationship:*
```
Camera-to-Subject Distance vs. Required Resolution:
10 feet: 720p adequate for detection
25 feet: 1080p needed for recognition  
50 feet: 4K necessary for identification
100+ feet: Multiple cameras or PTZ zoom required
```

**Coverage Patterns:**
- **Wide area surveillance:** sacrifices detail за broad coverage
- **Choke point monitoring:** high detail at specific locations
- **Perimeter protection:** motion detection along boundaries
- **Asset monitoring:** focused coverage на valuable items

**Environmental Factors:**
- **Weather resistance:** IP65/IP66 ratings за outdoor cameras
- **Temperature range:** operating conditions (-10°C to +50°C typical)
- **Vibration resistance:** mounting stability in windy conditions
- **Vandal resistance:** IK ratings за impact protection

**🔍 Advanced Features (4 мин)**

**Video Analytics:**
- **Motion detection:** automated alerts for movement в designated areas
- **Line crossing:** alerts when objects cross virtual boundaries
- **Loitering detection:** prolonged presence в restricted areas
- **Object removal:** alerts when items disappear от view

**Smart Camera Capabilities:**
- **On-board processing:** analytics performed at camera level
- **Edge computing:** reduced bandwidth, faster response times
- **Machine learning:** improving accuracy over time
- **Integration:** connecting with access control, alarm systems

### **ЧАС 4: Privacy, legal considerations и system integration (40 мин)**

#### **Теоретична część (20 мин):**

**⚖️ Legal Framework за Video Surveillance (8 мин)**

**GDPR Requirements (European Union):**
- **Lawful basis:** legitimate interest, consent, или legal obligation
- **Data minimization:** collect only necessary video data
- **Purpose limitation:** use video only за stated security purposes
- **Retention limits:** delete recordings after specified periods
- **Subject rights:** individuals can request access to footage на themselves

**Bulgarian Legislation:**
- **Закон за защита на личните данни:** national GDPR implementation
- **Закон за частна охранителна дейност:** private security regulations
- **Кодекс на труда:** workplace surveillance restrictions
- **Наказателно-процесуален кодекс:** video evidence admissibility

**Workplace Surveillance Rules:**
- **Employee notification:** workers must be informed на monitoring
- **Proportionality:** surveillance must be justified by security needs
- **Restricted areas:** no cameras в bathrooms, changing areas
- **Audio recording:** additional consent required за sound capture

**🔒 Privacy Protection Measures (6 мин)**

**Technical Privacy Controls:**
- **Privacy masking:** blocking specific areas от view (neighbor windows)
- **Face blurring:** automatic obscuring на faces в recorded video
- **Access control:** limiting who can view recordings
- **Audit trails:** logging who accessed what video when
- **Encryption:** protecting stored и transmitted video data

**Operational Procedures:**
- **Clear signage:** warning signs about video surveillance areas
- **Data handling policies:** who can access recordings, под what conditions
- **Retention schedules:** automatic deletion след specified periods
- **Incident procedures:** process за law enforcement requests

**Public vs. Private Surveillance:**
- **Public spaces:** lower expectation на privacy, greater surveillance latitude
- **Private property:** stronger privacy protections, consent requirements
- **Semi-public areas:** shopping malls, schools require careful balance

**🔗 System Integration (6 мин)**

**Access Control Integration:**
- **Event correlation:** linking camera recordings with card access events
- **Automatic tracking:** following individuals through facilities
- **Alarm verification:** visual confirmation на security breaches
- **Emergency response:** automated camera positioning during alarms

**Database Integration:**
- **Personnel records:** matching faces to employee databases
- **Visitor management:** tracking guest movements through facilities  
- **Incident reports:** linking video evidence to security incident records
- **Analytics data:** storing detection results за trend analysis

**Network Integration:**
- **Centralized management:** single interface за multiple security systems
- **Redundant communications:** backup connectivity за critical cameras
- **Bandwidth management:** QoS settings за video traffic priority
- **Remote monitoring:** secure access from mobile devices, remote centers

*Integration example: Corporate office building*
- Employee badges at entrance trigger camera recording
- Facial recognition confirms badge holder identity  
- Visitor without badge triggers alert и continuous tracking
- Emergency button activates PTZ cameras к specific location
- All events logged в central security management system
